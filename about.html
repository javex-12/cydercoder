Meet the human

I'm a full-stack product engineer based in Lagos. I build everything from polished marketing sites to internal Electron/Tauri tools, keeping documentation tidy and Results measurable for the teams I partner with.

How I work

Listening first

Every build starts with the people using it. I gather goals, note constraints, and make sure we solve the right problem before touching code.

Calm delivery

Short demos, async updates, and no surprise scope creep. Whether it's a SaaS dashboard or an Electron utility, you know what shipped and when.

Production-ready systems

Reusable UI kits, API contracts, and deployment pipelines that let applications like CySwitch, NaijaBot, and MentorConnect stay fast and maintainable.
